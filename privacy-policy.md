# Privacy Policy — Equilibrium

**Last updated: July 1, 2026**

Equilibrium ("the App") is a teacher wellness app built by Clear K-12. We take your privacy seriously.

## What we collect

When you create an account, we collect:
- Your **email address** — used to sign in and recover your account
- Your **first name** — used to personalize your experience

We also store the wellness data you create inside the app:
- Daily goal check-ins
- Water intake logs
- Journal entries
- Mood and energy ratings
- Streak history

## How we use it

We use your data **only to provide the app experience**. Specifically:
- To sync your data across devices
- To keep you signed in
- To send account-related emails (confirmation, password reset)

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to anyone
- Share your data with third parties for advertising
- Use your data to train AI models
- Send marketing emails

## Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely using [Supabase](https://supabase.com), a cloud database provider based in the United States. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.

## Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us at **kennady.nickell@gmail.com**, and we will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

## Children's privacy

Refill is designed for teachers (adults). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes, we'll notify you by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
